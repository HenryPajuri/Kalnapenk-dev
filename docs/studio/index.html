Vocal coaching

One-on-one vocal coaching, tailored to the singer in front of her. Beginners finding the voice for the first time, seasoned performers refining a specific technique, and everyone in between. Sessions in Estonian, English, or Spanish.

Voice therapy

A gentler practice for people who want to meet their voice rather than train it. Through breathwork, sound-making, and slow relaxing movement, she guides participants to the part of themselves that speaks and sings without performing. Held at Bikram Yoga Studio.
